Instruction

How to use:

  • Step 1: Buld Action List
    • Choose an action, select its level and any applicable modifiers based on your needs, then add it to the list.
    • Note: For troops, death damage always takes precedence over modifiers. If both are selected, death damage will be applied.
  • Step 2: Manage Action List
    • Navigate to the Action List section to view your list. You can see all your actions and their modifiers by clicking "Show Details".
    • You can remove the top 1 or 5 actions, or clear the entire list.
    • To remove a specific action or reorder the list:
      • Click on "Show Details".
      • To remove a specific action, click the trash can button.
      • To reorder the list, use the arrow button.
  • Step 3: Calculate
    • Navigate to the Defense List section to see the results.
    • Select the appropriate defense level based on your needs.
    • Each defense has a Detail section that shows:
      • How much damage each action inflicts.
      • The remaining HP of the defense after each action.
      • The impact of modifiers (e.g., Rage spell, cumulative Earthquake damage).
    • You can also search for a specific defense or hide certain defenses.

Detail table terminology:

  • Action:
    • An action is an offense that selected from the action list that will affect defense by deal damage or repair them.
    • For example: Fireball ðŸ”¥.
    • Note: The first row alway show the defense itself and its base HP.
  • Type:
    • The type of the action.
    • There are 2 types:
      • Attack: Deal damage to defense.
      • Repair: Restore defense's HP.
  • DPH/HPH (Damage Per Hit/Heal Per Hit): The amount of damage that the action deal to defense (or restore HP).
  • HP: The remaining HP of defense after action.

Note:

  • The damage log for each defense will stop once that defense's HP reaches 0 or below. After all, Builders can't repair a destroyed defense, right?
  • For performance reasons, the action list can hold a maximum of 30 actions.
  • All of your options are saved for future use, except for action list, which will reset when the page is refreshed.
    • You can clear it in Setting.
  • While the website is supported on small screens like phones, it's recommended to use a larger screen, such as a monitor or tablet, for the best experience.
